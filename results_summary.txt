Industrial Sound Anomaly Detection - Results Summary

Dataset:
https://zenodo.org/records/3384388

The dataset is not included in this repository because it is about 10 GB.

Compared Methods:
1. Method 1: Autoencoder anomaly detection
   - Trains only on normal audio.
   - Uses reconstruction error and latent-space distance.
   - Tunes reconstruction/latent weights and threshold on validation files.

2. Method 2: Supervised CNN classifier
   - Trains on normal and abnormal labels.
   - Predicts abnormal probability per chunk.
   - Uses top-k chunk probabilities for file-level prediction.

The tuned autoencoder improves recall, but it still produces many false positives.
The supervised CNN performs much better across all tested IDs, with high recall and low false positive rate.
